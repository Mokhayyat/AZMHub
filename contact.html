Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about AZM

How do I book a session with a mentor?
Booking a mentor session is simple! Browse our mentor directory, select a mentor that matches your interests, choose an available time slot, and complete the booking process. You'll receive a confirmation email with session details and a calendar invite.
What types of events does AZM organize?
AZM organizes three main types of events: Padel tournaments for networking and fun, educational workshops on various business topics, and networking events where you can connect with mentors and peers. All events are designed to facilitate meaningful connections and learning.
How much do mentor sessions cost?
Mentor session prices vary based on the mentor's experience and expertise, typically ranging from $50-$200 per session. We also offer package deals and some free introductory sessions. Event pricing varies from free networking events to paid workshops and tournaments.
Can I become a mentor on AZM?
Yes! We're always looking for experienced professionals to join our mentor community. The application process includes a background check, experience verification, and an interview. Successful applicants receive training and support to provide the best mentoring experience.
Are the podcasts available on other platforms?
Yes! Our podcasts are available on all major platforms including Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Google Podcasts, and more. You can also listen directly on our website with our custom audio player that includes additional features like episode notes and related resources.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule a mentor session?
You can cancel or reschedule sessions up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without penalty. Simply log into your account, go to your bookings, and select the cancel or reschedule option. For cancellations within 24 hours, please contact our support team.
